# Responsible AI

## Safety principles

- Use simulated content only.
- State that responses do not approve or deny benefits.
- Avoid collecting complete sensitive data.
- Escalate high-risk or vulnerable cases.
- Log only masked conversation summaries.
- Keep a human advisor in the loop for sensitive decisions.

## Abuse resistance

The agent blocks or redirects requests involving passwords, bank accounts, complete document numbers, benefit approval guarantees, or unsupported topics.

## Human oversight

High-risk cases produce a safe handoff summary with category, risk level, user need, recommended next step, and human advisor requirement.
